Europe ｜ 欧洲


Europa, referred to as Europe, is a continent located in the northwest of Eurasia, with an area of approximately 10,180,000 square kilometers and a total population of 748,571,960 (as of the end of July 2022). It is the third most populous continent in the world, second only to Asia and Africa, a total of 50 independent sovereign states. Europe is bounded by the Ural Mountains and the Ural River in the east, Asia by the Caspian Sea, the Caucasus Mountains and the Black Sea in the southeast, North America in the west and northwest by the Atlantic Ocean, the Greenland Sea, and the Denmark Strait, the Arctic Sea in the north, and Africa in the south by the Mediterranean Sea. The northernmost tip of Europe is the North Cape of Norway, and the southernmost tip is Cape Marocchi in Spain. Europe is the second smallest continent in the world, only slightly larger than Oceania. It and Asia are collectively called Eurasia, and together with Asia, Africa is collectively called the Eurasian and African continents.

欧罗巴洲，简称欧洲，是位于欧亚大陆西北部的一个洲，面积约10,180,000平方千米，总人口达748,571,960（截止2022年7月底），世界人口第三多的洲，仅次于亚洲和非洲，共有50个已独立的主权国家。欧洲东以乌拉山脉、乌拉河，东南以里海、高加索山脉和黑海与亚洲为界，西、西北隔大西洋、格陵兰海、丹麦海峡与北美洲相望，北接北极海，南隔地中海与非洲相望。欧陆最北端是挪威的北角，最南端是西班牙的马罗基角，欧洲是世界上第二小的洲、大陆，仅比大洋洲大一些，其与亚洲合称为欧亚大陆，而与亚洲、非洲合称为欧亚非大陆。
